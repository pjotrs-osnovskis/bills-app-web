Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Effective from: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Qlynton ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses and protects your personal data when you use our invoicing service. By using Qlynton, you agree to this policy.

1. Who We Are

Qlynton is an invoicing platform for freelancers and small teams. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data We Collect

When you sign in and use Qlynton, we collect:

  • Account data: Your name, email address and profile picture provided by Google Sign-In.
  • Business data: Company details, banking information, client names and contact details, services and invoice data that you enter into the application.
  • Usage data: Application settings and preferences stored on our servers.
  • Technical data: Session information, IP address and browser type for authentication and security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and operate the Qlynton invoicing service
  • Authenticate your identity via Google OAuth
  • Store and retrieve your invoices, clients and company data
  • Generate PDF invoices from your data
  • Improve and develop the service

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Processing is necessary to provide you with the service.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): Security, fraud prevention and service improvement.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): Where required by applicable law.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely. We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption in transit and secure server-side storage. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: For typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

As an EU resident, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Export your data in a machine-readable format (use Settings → Export JSON)
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Restriction — Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

8. Cookies

Qlynton uses only essential session cookies required for authentication and security. We do not use tracking, advertising or analytics cookies. No cookie consent banner is required for strictly necessary cookies under GDPR.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Qlynton after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
